  • Can't Take Us Alive

    Mayhem's June 8, 2018 debut was marked by the release of their mini album titled “Can't Take Us Alive”. A compilation of 5 tracks in total, the album depicts a world in which dreams have been swept away to be replaced by the towering presence of reality. The boys' story opens with ‘Maniac’ and closes with ‘Not today’, narrating how together they make things possible that was impossible alone.

    1. Maniac
    2. RED
    3. Really really
    4. Crown
    5. Not today

  • BLACKOUT

    MAYHEM continues their journey through the newest comeback album on March 4, 2019. In “Blackout” the experience of a first breakup strikes as a powerful storm mixed with denial, dejection, anger, and loss. The album is a post-farewell recollection with six tracks total.

    1. 24
    2. Maze in the mirror
    3. Good lil boy
    4. Rebel yell
    5. Careful
    6. Hold on

  • NOT FOUND

    Mayhem's second comeback album released on 15 September, 2020; depicts youth on the brink of adulthood. After experiencing conflict and chaos, the boys feel a desire to postpone their growth and linger in the freedom of Neverland. Their journey forward despite this desire is a representation of today's generation as they continue to grow through the challenges of the world. It consists of five tracks in total, includying their digital single ‘Find a Way’.

    1. Find a Way
    2. Escape
    3. Farewell Neverland
    4. Cheese
    5. Save me

  • Find a Way

    MAYHEM's first digital single released on 2 February, 2020.

    In a world where their values begin to collide, they recognize their desires for the first time and begin to question their identity. Hands tied in their new dilemma, they refuse to accept what is labeled as the “correct answer”—showing determination to focus on the present and find a way to get the answers while living life on their own terms.

  • Be The Change

    MAYHEM's second digital single released on 14 January, 2021. With an unexpected change in their characteristic palette, the once black and red group brings forward their strength and optimism, showing the world can hit them but they will never give up, getting free from any shackles.
