Meet the band

MAYHEM has come together under one dream in hopes of building a new era, delivering an energetic blend of dance-pop and hip-hop with deeply introspective lyrics.

Formed by producer Ji Chang Wook, under the firm LOST IN TRANSLATION inside High Class Entertainment, the korean boy band consists of four members, Minsoo, D.Min, Wyld and Dae; all shining in their own way to create this beautiful synergy.

They debuted on June 8th, 2018 with the mini-album titled "Can't Take Us Alive".
